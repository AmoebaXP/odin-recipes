Description

This recipe is going to help you create the best vegan spaghetti you've ever eaten! It's going to bring together Impossible Sausage, vegan parmesan cheese crumbles, and tomato sauce simmered with garden vegetables. I think you're going to love it!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Impossible Sausage

  • Green Bell Peppers
