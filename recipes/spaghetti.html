Vegan Spaghetti Recipe

Back to Home



Description

This recipe is going to help you create the best vegan spaghetti you've ever eaten! It's going to bring together Impossible Sausage, vegan parmesan cheese crumbles, and tomato sauce simmered with garden vegetables. I think you're going to love it!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Impossible Sausage
  • Vegan Parmesan Cheese Crumbles
  • Prego Spaghetti Sauce
  • Green Bell Peppers
  • Chopped Cilantro

Steps

  1. Boil water and insert spaghetti noodles
  2. Fry Impossible Sausage in oil and spices of your choice
  3. Simmer green bell peppers in Prego spaghetti sauce
  4. Strain noodles and palce back in pot on medium-low heat
  5. Pour spaghetti sauce with bell peppers into your noodles
  6. Place Impossible Sausage in your noodles
  7. Stir your pot!
  8. Sprinkle Vegan Parmesean and Cilantro on top.
  9. Enjoy!
