Vegan Stuffing Recipe

Back to Home



Description

This recipe is going to help you create the best vegan stuffing you've ever eaten! It's going to bring together whole grain bread, vegetable broth, green lentils, onions, celery, sage, and flaxseed meal. I think you're going to love it!

Ingredients

  • Whole-grain Bread
  • Vegetable Broth
  • Vegan Butter
  • Green Lentils
  • Flaxseed Meal
  • Onions
  • Celery
  • Sage

Steps

  1. Boil water and insert spaghetti noodles
  2. Fry Impossible Sausage in oil and spices of your choice
  3. Simmer green bell peppers in Prego spaghetti sauce
  4. Strain noodles and palce back in pot on medium-low heat
  5. Pour spaghetti sauce with bell peppers into your noodles
  6. Place Impossible Sausage in your noodles
  7. Stir your pot!
  8. Sprinkle Vegan Parmesean and Cilantro on top.
  9. Enjoy!
