Vegan Funeral Potaoes Recipe

Back to Home



Description

This recipe is going to help you create the best vegan funeral potatoes you've ever eaten! It's going to bring together hashbrowns, vegan sour cream, cream of mushroom soup, cornflakes, vegan cheddar cheese, and deliciously chopped chives. I think you're going to love it!

Ingredients

  • Hashbrowns
  • Vegan Sour Cream
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • Crushed Cornflakes
  • Vegan Cheddar Cheese
  • Chopped Chives

Steps

  1. Thaw hashbrowns and put them in casserole dish
  2. Bring mushroom soup to a boil and drop to low heat
  3. (Optional): Strain mushrooms out of soup to use as a side
  4. Pur mushroom soup into casserole dish with hashbrowns
  5. Place some vegan sour cream in the casserole dish
  6. Fold ingredients together in the dish!
  7. Sprinkle vegan chedder cheese and crushed cornflakes on top
  8. Bake at 425 degrees for 30 minutes
  9. Enjoy!
